How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to request deletion of your SOSE account and all associated data:

1 Send a deletion request

Click the button below or send an email to 108city@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my SOSE account".

2 Include your sign-in email

In the email body, include the Google email address you use to sign in to SOSE so we can locate your account.

3 Receive confirmation

We will process your request and send a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted. Requests are processed within 30 days.

Request Account Deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

Data Type Action Retention Period
Account profile (email, name) Deleted Removed within 30 days
Habits and blocks Deleted Removed within 30 days
Completion records and streaks Deleted Removed within 30 days
Subscription and trial status Deleted Removed within 30 days
Google Play purchase records Retained by Google Managed by Google per their policies

Note: Google Play purchase and transaction records are managed by Google and are subject to Google's privacy policy. SOSE does not have the ability to delete these records.

Important Information

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All habit data, streaks, and progress will be lost
  • Any active premium subscription will be cancelled
  • You will need to cancel your Google Play subscription separately via the Play Store to stop future billing
