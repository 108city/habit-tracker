SOSE - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Introduction

SOSE ("Sum Of Small Efforts") is a habit tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

When you use SOSE, we collect the following:

  • Account information: Your Google account email address and display name, collected when you sign in with Google OAuth.
  • Habit data: The habits you create, completion records, streaks, and block information you enter into the app.
  • Subscription status: Whether you are on a free or premium plan, and trial expiry dates.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the habit tracking service
  • To sync your habits across devices
  • To manage your account and subscription

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Your data is stored in a database protected by row-level security, meaning only you can access your own data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: Used for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to the sign-in process.
  • Supabase: Used for data storage and authentication. See Supabase's privacy policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not display ads

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us and we will remove your data within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your habit data

Children's Privacy

SOSE is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
